ABOUT US

A global leader in independent ATM networks, TecBan has been investing and innovating continuously for over 40 years, developing technological solutions that seamlessly integrate the physical and digital realms, driving the economic ecosystem of Brazil to become increasingly efficient. As a visionary company, TecBan is always in motion. In the last decade alone, it has invested over R$ 4 billion in advanced products and solutions that contribute to ensuring financial inclusion for all social classes and audience profiles in every region of Brazil. TecBan offers solutions such as ATMManager, Open Banking as a Service TecBan, and HubDigital TecBan. The company also operates in the telecommunications and valuables transportation segments through TBNet and TBForte.
